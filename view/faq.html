FAQ

Q: How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

A: At http://print.hz.nl can you log in with your HZ account and upload a file that you want to print, using your HZ pas by any HZ printers you will be able print them.

Q: How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?

A: At a HZ printer you can scan your pas and choose scan and the files will be send to your mail box.
