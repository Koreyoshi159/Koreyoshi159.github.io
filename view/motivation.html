Theory

Motivation is generally regarded as an important component for success, in school and at work. According to Kendra Cheery "What is Motivation?", there is two types of motivation: Extrinsic motivations and Intrinsic motivations. Extrinsic motivation comes from outside the person and often involve rewards, Intrinsic motivation comes from the person self and doing it for self-pleasure.
